Account Deletion Request

Last updated: 7 January 2026

App Name: 11+LearnWords

Developer: Big Test Solutions Limited

We value your privacy. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data (including learning progress, stars, and collected words) from 11+LearnWords, you have two options:

Option 1: Delete via App (Instant)

1. Open the 11+LearnWords app.

2. Navigate to the Parent Zone tab.

3. Tap on Delete Account.

4. Confirm the deletion.

Option 2: Delete via Web Request

If you have uninstalled the app or cannot access it, please contact us to request manual deletion.

Email: support@bigtestsolutions.com

Please provide your registered email address. We will process your request within 7 business days.

Data Retention

To ensure we do not hold your data indefinitely, accounts that are inactive for more than 1 year will be automatically deleted along with all associated data. You can keep your account active simply by logging in.

Note: This action is irreversible. All your progress will be permanently lost.
